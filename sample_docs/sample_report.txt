QUARTERLY ANALYSIS REPORT
-------------------------
Department: Operations
Author: Vineel Sai Reddy
Date: June 12, 2026
Title: Automation Opportunities in Customer Onboarding

Executive Summary:
This report highlights key areas where the customer onboarding pipeline can be optimized using Generative AI and automated workflows.

Key Findings:
- Manual document verification takes an average of 4.5 minutes per customer.
- Implementation of LLM-based structured extraction could reduce this time to under 15 seconds.
- Support tickets related to missed onboarding steps have increased by 12% in Q2.

Recommendations:
1. Integrate LangGraph-based verification pipelines immediately.
2. Automate webhook triggers from the CRM to our processing endpoints.
3. Replace the legacy regex parser with prompt-engineered LLM calls for ID extraction.
